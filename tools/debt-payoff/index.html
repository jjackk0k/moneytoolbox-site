How it works

Avalanche: pay minimums on everything, then throw every extra dollar at the highest-APR debt first. Mathematically saves the most interest.

Snowball: attack the smallest balance first. Costs a little more interest but gives faster wins, which helps many people stick with it.

Estimates assume fixed APRs and on-time payments, interest compounded monthly. Educational tool, not financial advice.
